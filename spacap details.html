Product Description

A "spa cap" typically refers to a protective cover placed over a hot tub or spa cover, designed to shield it from the elements like rain, sun, and debris, usually made from a durable, water-resistant material and often featuring a fitted design to securely cover the entire spa surface; it can also sometimes refer to a cap used to cover the inlet and outlet pipes of a spa to prevent water leakage or debris from entering the system.
